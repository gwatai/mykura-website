Overview

A Remote Election Management & Voting System

Are you looking for an Election Solution?

Mykura's innovative solution for remote election management and voting addresses the issues of transparency, efficiency, and accessibility within the electoral process, by providing a secure and reliable platform for remote voting, election management & tallying. A solution that is not just a Voting Website, get an Election Management Portal, Voters Portal (Web-vote), USSD-Voting and Realtime Election results Webpage. Mykura Voting software is also integrated with SMS & EMAIL for communication to voters to aid in increasing their participation, VVPAT, and addition of a Soft-ballot printing system to ensure a verifiable election.
